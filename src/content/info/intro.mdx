Swiftology is an investigative blog where I explore and analyze iOS development concepts. It's a space for deep dives, real-time learning, and personal insights into Swift and iOS.

### Features

- **In-Depth Exploration**: Thorough analysis of iOS concepts, from common patterns to obscure frameworks.
- **Practical Examples**: Code examples that show real-world application of each topic.
- **Iterative Learning**: Posts are updated with new insights and improved understanding over time.
- **Unpacking My Process**: Transparent look at the mistakes and breakthroughs during the learning process.
- **Hands-On Investigation**: Testing different approaches, frameworks, and performance implications.
- **Personal Insights**: My thoughts on trends, features, and opinions in the iOS ecosystem.
- **Swift & iOS Focused**: Dedicated to deepening your expertise in Swift and iOS development.
